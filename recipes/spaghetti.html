Spaghetti

Description

Classic fan favorite for those nights where you want to be lazy. I fully support buying pre-made marinara sauce. And frozen garlic bread. Don't make anything yourself except for the pasta.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Frozen garlic bread
  • Maybe some parm

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti. Drain.
  2. Heat up marinara sauce in a pot on the stove.
  3. Heat up garlic bread in the oven per instructions.
  4. Throw the noods in a bowl, top with marinara & parm and serve with side of garlic bread.
