Brussels Sprouts Hash

Description

A delicious breakfast fried rice with farro, brussels sprouts, bacon, and chermoula. Super easy to put together, most of your time is spent preparing vegetables. Make your own chermoula or buy some green hot sauce - either way, this dish is sure to be a new family favorite!

Ingredients

  • Farro
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Bacon
  • Sweet onion
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Brussels sprouts
  • Sesame seeds
  • Fried eggs
  • Green hot sauce

Steps

  1. Cook farro. Drain.
  2. Season sweet potatoes then roast.
  3. Cook bacon in skillet. Add onion, then garlic & thyme.
  4. Add brussels sprouts & cook until bright green.
  5. Add in farro & roasted sweet potatoes until farro is warm.
  6. Fry two eggs. Throw 'em on top of the hash & serve.
